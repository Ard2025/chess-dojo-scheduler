Time Controls - We've heard your plight and our heart strings were pulled ð. We've adjusted the minimum classical time controls to the following:

  • Under 800: 30+0
  • 800-1200: 30+30
  • 1200-1600: 45+30
  • 1600-2000: 60+30
  • 2000+: 90+30

These are our minimum suggested time controls. Note that you can also play an alternate time control as long as the base time + increment adds up to the same or higher number that we've suggested. E.g. for 60+30 (which adds up to 90), 45+45 would also be acceptable, as well as 75+15, 85+5, etc. as long as you have a minimum starting time of 30 minutes (you cannot play 1+90 or 15+15 to have it count for classical).

We would like to reiterate that if you have a tournament which falls below our minimum, you can still play and get a lot out of it! But we do need to offer a firm guideline for what counts for Dojo Points and what doesn't.


Average Rating Change per Dojo Point - On the Statistics Page you can now see the average rating change per dojo point. Across the Dojo the number is now 2.04. So if you gain just one Dojo point your expected rating gain is 2. That's a two for one! We are working on getting rid of the statistical outliers and expect the graph to smooth out once that is done.


Free Tier - In case you missed it last month, we now offer a completely free version of the program to get an overall feel for the site. You get access to a few tasks per category, the activity and time tracker, the ability to book calendar events and more. Sign-up for free at dojoscoreboard.com.
